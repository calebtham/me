About me

I am currently a University of Warwick student studying Computer Science MEng.

I have always been captivated by the pervasiveness of technology. Reading of the field's exiting applications at an early age spurred me to pursue a career in Software Engineering. As I started to learn how to program, I immediately fell in love with the mix of problem-solving and creative thinking. Areas I'm particularly passionate about are machine learning and full stack development. I now find myself coding for my studies, for business, and for fun!

In my spare time, I enjoy continuing to express my creative side through composing music, photography, and graphic design - even still I find myself glued to a screen, using software such as Logic Pro and Adobe's Creative Suite. I love sharing my passions, and so I became Head of Marketing at Warwick Jazz Society.
